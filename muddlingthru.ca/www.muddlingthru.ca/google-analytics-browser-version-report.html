Google Analytics Browser Version Report

Tags: web-development, google-analytics

Thursday, August 13, 2015 5:17:10 PM

  1. Login to Google Analytics.
  2. Click on the analytics “View” that you want to inspect.
  3. In the main, horizontal navigation bar, choose “Customization.”
  4. Then choose “+ New Custom Report.”
  5. Add appropriate dimensions (browser, browser version) and metrics (sessions.)
  6. When you click Save, you can view the report by clicking it in the left side nav.
