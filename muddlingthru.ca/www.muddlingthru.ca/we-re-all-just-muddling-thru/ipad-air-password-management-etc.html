Problem / Goal List

  1. How do we save passwords in the iPad and in general? We can choose between using the Apple built-in password manager OR choose something that's more generic such as LastPass. 
  2. How can we dictate an email with the iPad air? 
  3. How do we download music from iTunes? Go through the iTunes store. See below. DONE.
  4. How to download music from CDs?  This will require having a CD drive, which will either be part of your existing desktop/laptop computer. Alternatively, you might be able to just use an external CD drive, which will cost around $50.
  5. How do we read books with Overdrive? This is a general method for reading library eBooks (etc) that works with many libraries and many computer devices. It's worth learning. We can teach you. 
  6. Can we setup the iPad to play music directly to a hearing aid? 
  7. Attach images to email from the iPad.  
  8. Install a dictionary app on the iPad. In particilar, the Oxford dictionary would be good. Use the AppStore not the iTunes store to download apps. DONE.
  9. Read the favorite news from the iPad. For both the dictionary and the news apps, you would get thoses from the App Store. Alternatively, you can access both a dictionary and the news from a web browser. DONE.

How do we download music from iTunes.

  1. Connect to the Internet (e.g. through ShawOpen). 
  2. Open iTunes (i.e. the iTunes store).
  3. Find the song that you want (i.e. select 'Music', then search). 
  4. Download the song (i.e. click the price of the song, then add your password to confirm).
  5. Find and listen to the song on your tablet (i.e. open 'Music', choose 'Songs', click the song). 
  6. Turn off the music (click the pause button and then the home button).
