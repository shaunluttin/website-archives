iPad Goals

Tags: 2013-119SN, ipad

Thursday, May 8, 2014 11:54:16 PM

Make email accessible by Outlook App on the laptop. Done.

Make email accessible by only the Shaw Webmail iPad (i.e. not the Mac Mail program). Done. Settings > Mail > Choose the account > Delete Account.

Access the Shaw Customer Center. Done.

Access Facebook from both the laptop and the iPad. Done. We reset the password.

  • E.g. on the iPad…
  • Open Safari.
  • Type facebook into the address/search bar.
  • The search results will display.
  • Ignore the crap at the top of the page.
  • Find the Facebook search result.
  • Either click the title of the result, or click the Login sub option of the result.
  • Facebook.com should open.

Respond to the message “You are not connected to the Internet” on the iPad. I.e. Connect to the Internet. Done.

  • Click Settings
  • Click Wi-Fi (on the left)
  • The Wi-Fi area will open on the right.
  • If there is a network (e.g. ShawOpen) listed with a green tick, then you are connected to a network.
  • If not, then you have to connect.

Setup and use OverDrive to read eBooks from the library.

Change an item that is in the Amazon cart just before paying.
