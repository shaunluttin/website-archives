Output the number of Git commits a person made each day of the year in bash.

It's the end of the year. Sometimes I want to know how many commits I made per day. This also helps me to see how many days I worked per year. (The unrelated photo is of the recycling centre at our cafe).

git --no-pager log --author=shaun --format='%ad' --date=format:'commits on %a %d %b %Y' --after 2020 | uniq -c

Here is the reference for the date formats:

  • https://strftime.org/
  • https://stackoverflow.com/a/19742762
