How do we access the Salt Spring Island Public Library with a Kindle HD?

Friday, November 15, 2013 6:16:00 PM

Setup Overdrive Media Console on Your Kindle Fire

  1. Download the Overdrive Media Console App (OMC App). I.e. Open your Kindle and go to > Home > Shop > Search > ... Then search for and download the OMC App.
  2. Install the OMC App. Once you have downloaded it to your Kindle, go to > Home > Apps > Cloud > Overdrive Media Console to install it.
  3. Create an Adobe ID. It's easiest to do this here: http://www.adobe.com/content/apex/en/home/reg/reg1.html from a computer with a keyboard, but you can also do it from the OMC App later.
  4. Open the OMC App. Kindle > Home > Apps > Device > Overdrive Media Console.
  5. Associate your Adobe ID with Overdrive. Do this in OMC App Settings.
  6. Yah! You've setup OMC.

Add a Library and Use a Book

  1. Open the OMC App. See above.
  2. Add the Salt Spring Island Public Library to Overdrive.
  3. Download a book.
  4. Open the book. Note: You will find the book in the OMC App Bookshelf (not in the Kindle Bookshelf).
  5. Close the book.
  6. Return the book.
