What is the process of downloading and using an app on a Android tablet.*

  • Use Google Play (aka Play Store) to download apps that have gone through the Google quality control process.
  • There are other ways to install apps, but they may or may not be of quality or safe.
  • Google play provide compatibilty, safety, and quality control.

Google Play Interface

  • Home Page. This is like the show room and is equivalent of annoying sales people. Avoid.
  • Apps > Categories. This lets you browse the categories of the apps. Recommended.
  • Apps > Top Free.  This lets you view the most popular free apps. Recommended.
  • Search Play. This lets you filter all the apps by keywords. Results are usually sorted by relevance. When you search start with more broad terms and specify. For instance, start with TV, then TV sports, then TV sports soccer. Recommended.

Google Play - Find, Install, Use, Unintall

  1. Open the Play Store.
  2. Click Search (which is the magnifying glass in the top right corner).
  3. Type your search term (e.g. Dictionary)
  4. Find an app that you like and click it's icon.
  5. On the apps page click the INSTALL button.
  6. Accept the permission (usually they are fine, but you can read them if you have concerns).
  7. The app will start to download onto your computer from the Internet.
  8. Go home.
  9. Find the icon that the app put on your screen.
  10. Click the icon to open the app.
  11. Once you've checkout out the app, go back home.
  12. Visit your device's Settings menu > Apps or Application manager (this may differ depending on your device).
  13. Touch the app you’d like to uninstall.
  14. Select Uninstall.
