How to print a sub-link?

Some websites are not designed for print. That is, they are designed, for example, to look good on a laptop but not on a printed piece of paper. In this case, you have two options:

  1. Take a screen shot and then print that.
  2. Copy the link and include the steps to recreate the view that you want to share.

How can I listen to books on my iPad?

There are two options here:

  1. Buy or borrow and listen to the audio book. This is great because voice actors or the author skillfully reads the book.
  2. Buy the eBook and listen with a screen reader. This is not a good experience, because the screen reader will sound robotic.

To obtain an audio book, search your local library (you'll probably need OverDrive), try http://www.amazon.ca, or browse through the iTunes store. Talk to your librarian to learn how to install Overdrive on your iPad.

How to change font size, spacing, and style in MS Word?

This depends on the version of MS Word that you are using. Try searching Google with the following queries:

  • ms word 2003 formatting toolbar
  • ms word 2003 formatting toolbar youtube
  • ms word 2003 show hide toolbar
  • ms word 2003 show hide toolbar youtube
  • keep searching until you find what you're wanting

Also, here is a particularly good tutorial: http://www.wikihow.com/Add-Toolbars-to-Microsoft-Word
