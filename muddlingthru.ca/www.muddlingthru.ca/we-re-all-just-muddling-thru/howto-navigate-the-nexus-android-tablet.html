HowTo: Navigate the Nexus Android tablet

Friday, January 24, 2014 7:18:00 PM

Access Shaw Go WiFi

Usually when you're in a Shw Go WiFi zone, this is a three step process, but for the Android you need a forth step to manually access the sign on page: 

  1. Open the WiFi settings on the Android. 
  2. Select "ShawOpen" from the list of wireless networks.
  3. Open your web browser (e.g. Chrome / Firefox).
  4. Navigate to https://wifisignon.shaw.ca

Usually step (4) is not necessary. At step (3) Shaw is supposed to prompt you to sign in with you email address. On the Andriod, though, you need step (4). Wierd.

Save a contact from the Gmail App

While there is a shortcut for this in the Gmail Web App, there is NOT a shortcut for this in the Gmail Android App. Instead, do the copy and paste technique: 

  1. Expand the contact in the email. 
  2. Touch and hold the contacts email address link. 
  3. A "Copy" prompt will show in the upper right corner. 
  4. Click copy. 
  5. Go back to Android home. 
  6. Open your contacts. 
  7. Create new contact. 
  8. Touch and hold the input field for email. 
  9. A paste prompt will show. 
  10. Click paste.
