How do I clean my screen? Use 1/2 and 1/2 water and rubbing alchohol. If you have it, use de-mineralized water (personally, I just use tap water or filtered water).

How do I close an app on a touchscreen, when that app doesn't have a red X? 

  1. You don't have to close the app. Just do something else, and the app will sleep in the background.
  2. If you want to close it manually though, drag it from the top all the way down to the bottom of the screen (if a menu comes down from the top, just keep dragging). See this video for more details: http://windows.microsoft.com/en-ca/windows-8/how-close-app

How do I install a new app?

  • There are two types of apps: native apps and web apps.
  • Native apps run directly on your computer. You install these from the App Store.
  • Web apps run within a web browser. You find these with a web search engine. Web browsers include Google Chome, Microsoft Internet Explorer, and Mozilla Firefox, Apple Safari. All of these are very good web browsers. Once you choose your favorite web browser, you can then learn to bookmark your favorite apps. You can also set certain browser to open with a list of your favorites.

How do I program (or bookmark) a new web app in Google Chrome?

  • The easiest way to do this, is with a Extension called Speed Dial 2, because it makes your bookmarks available on your start page. We installed this during your tutoring session. We also added your nine existing bookmarks, which you had already bookmarked. 
  • To add a new bookmark, take the following steps: 
  • 1. Open Google Chrome. 
  • 2. Your start page will show with all your bookmarks. 
  • 3. Click on the Google bookmark.
  • 4. Type in "coronation street," for example. 
  • 5. Click on one of the search results. 
  • 6. If you like the page, then bookmark it. 
  • 7. Click the lightning bolt in the top right of the page. 
  • 8. A menu will display that says, "Save this page to:"
  • 9. Click/tap on "Home"
  • 10. You will know you've succeeded if it says, "Saved to speed dial," in blue font.
  • Now if you close and reopen Google Chrome, the page will in your list of bookmarks.
