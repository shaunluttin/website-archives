Purpose the First

  • Purpose - To educate people in Computer Literacy, to occupy youth in Salt Spring Island with educational activities.
  • Activities in support of Purpose - Computer Programming Training at the Salt Spring Public Library Meeting rooms and/or Program Room when available. These are sixteen week programs that run from 10 am until noon every Friday. They involve teaching the basics of computer programming including: setting up a version control system for collaboration (Git and GitHub), programming HTML, CSS, and JavaScript in the browser, programming REST and C# for client/server communication, programming Python for RPi functionality. The staff include people who are competent in computer programming and interested in learning more. Currently, Shaun Luttin is teaching these programs.
  • Activities in support of Purpose - Computer Hardware Collection, Refurbishment, and Distribution. Salt Spring Island. This involves soliciting donations of used computers, fixing up the computers so that students can use them for learning computer programming, and distributing them to students who need them. Staff will include people who are competent with computer hardware and operating systems. Shaun Luttin has already started to do this.
  • Activities in support of Purpose – Computer Literacy Talks…

Purpose the Second

  • Purpose - To educate people in the performance art of magic, to increase confidence and performance abilities, to provide youth programs that are educational and confidence building.
  • Activities in support of Purpose - Salt Spring Conjuring Club that meets at the Salt Spring Public Library Meeting rooms and/or Program Room when available. It includes Teaching the basics of sleight of hand. Teaching the basics of showmanship. Teaching the basics of goal setting. Teaching the basics of audience attention control. Putting on a public show once a term (i.e. every four months). Staff include People who are interested in magic as a performance art. Shaun Luttin currently is teaching these programs. Eric Jacobsen is currently building the stage on which we will perform.
  • Activities in support of Purpose - Public Performances at community events in Salt Spring Island. This involves setting up a small performing area at public events, attending the event with enough performance material, and then performing at the community event.
