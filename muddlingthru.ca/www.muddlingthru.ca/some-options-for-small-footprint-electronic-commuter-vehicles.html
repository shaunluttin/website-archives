Some options for small-footprint, electronic, commuter vehicles?

Wednesday, November 20, 2013 4:06:00 AM

Utility by Dynasty Electric Car Manufacturer.

  • Low Speed Electric Vehicle (LSEV or LSV)
  • can operate on roads with posted speed limits of up to 60kph
  • at a speed not to exceed 40kph (i.e. max speed 40kph)
  • approx $18,000 (too expensive)

GEM 2014 e2

  • LSEV
  • max speed 40 kph
  • approx $8,000 (nice price!)

Smart Four Two

  • Full Performance Electric Vehicle (FPEV)
  • max speed 125 kph (too fast, no thanks)
  • approx $27,000
