Hidden Way to Register Quickbooks Pro 2013

Tags: quickbooks

Wednesday, July 16, 2014 12:51:02 AM

I was having lots of trouble registering QuickBooks Pro. The following method worked for me, and I wanted to share it.

  • Open Quickbooks
  • (Skip the normal registration screen, if necessary).
  • Once Quickbooks opens, choose Help > About Quickbooks Pro
  • While the About Window is still open, press Ctrl + R + P
  • Follow the instructions in the super special registration window.
