Type at 80 words per minute.

Tuesday, August 27, 2013 5:06:00 PM

I would like to type with 100% accuracy at 80 WPM. This requires measurable goals and typing software.

For typing software, I chose Rapid Typing Tutor from the download.com website. It is usable, simple, and fun.

http://download.cnet.com/Rapid-Typing-Tutor/3000-2051_4-10666000.html

For measurable goals, I have chosen to work through the following Lessons. The lesson is done if I achieve 80 WPM with 100% accuracy (backspace is OK) on each section in one sitting.
